Some Non-Blog Tilde Sites

I am particularly interested in creative, non-blog[1] tilde projects. A non-blog, for my purposes, is a site that doesn’t serve primarily as a chronological record of (mostly) personal/narrative/confessional entries. This is a very wide category and I don’t mean this list to be exhaustive. These are just the few I know and like. I’d very much welcome pointers to other content-laden non-blogs. I plan to keep curating this list. At the moment, it is only a tiny, initial selection drawn from some of the neighboring tilde.townies.
